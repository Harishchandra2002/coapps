Employee Onboarding

New Employee Registration

  • New employee receives an email invitation to join the HRMS software.
  • Employee clicks on the registration link provided in the email.
  • Employee fills out the registration form with personal information (name, email, address, etc.).
  • Employee uploads required documents (ID proof, resume, etc.).
  • Employee clicks on the "Submit" button.

Manager Approval

  • HR manager receives a notification about the new employee registration.
  • HR manager reviews the registration details and documents.
  • HR manager approves the registration request.
  • Employee receives a confirmation email upon approval.

Access to Employee Portal

  • Employee receives login credentials (username and password) via email.
  • Employee logs in to the HRMS software using the provided credentials.
  • Employee gains access to the employee portal/dashboard.

Leave Management

Requesting Leave

  • Employee logs in to the HRMS software.
  • Employee navigates to the "Leave" section.
  • Employee clicks on the "Request Leave" button.
  • Employee selects the type of leave (sick leave, vacation, etc.).
  • Employee selects the start and end dates of the leave.
  • Employee provides a reason for the leave.
  • Employee submits the leave request.

Manager Approval

  • Manager receives a notification about the leave request.
  • Manager reviews the request details.
  • Manager approves or denies the leave request.
  • Employee receives a notification about the status of their leave request.

Viewing Leave Balance

  • Employee navigates to the "Leave" section.
  • Employee views their remaining leave balance for different leave types.
  • Employee can also view their leave history.

Performance Management

Setting Goals

  • Manager and employee meet to discuss and set performance goals.
  • Manager logs in to the HRMS software.
  • Manager navigates to the "Performance" section.
  • Manager creates performance goals for the employee.
  • Manager assigns goals and sets deadlines.

Performance Review

  • At the end of the performance cycle, manager conducts a performance review.
  • Manager evaluates employee performance based on predefined criteria.
  • Manager provides feedback and ratings for each goal.
  • Employee receives feedback and ratings from the manager.

Development Plan

  • Based on the performance review, manager and employee collaborate on a development plan.
  • Manager assigns training programs, courses, or projects to help the employee improve.

Payroll Management

Generating Payroll

  • HR manager logs in to the HRMS software.
  • HR manager navigates to the "Payroll" section.
  • HR manager inputs employee attendance, leaves, and other relevant data.
  • HR manager verifies and approves payroll calculations.
  • HR manager generates payroll for the pay period.

Employee Payslip

  • Employees receive email notifications about the generation of their payslips.
  • Employees log in to the HRMS software to view and download their payslips.
  • Employees can view details such as salary, deductions, taxes, etc.

Recruitment and Applicant Tracking

Job Posting

  • HR manager logs in to the HRMS software.
  • HR manager navigates to the "Recruitment" section.
  • HR manager creates a new job posting by providing job details, requirements, and qualifications.
  • HR manager publishes the job posting to various job boards and platforms.

Candidate Application

  • Job seekers find the job posting on job boards or the company website.
  • Candidates apply for the job by submitting their resume and other required documents through the HRMS software.
  • Candidates receive a confirmation email upon successful submission.

Application Review

  • HR manager receives notifications about new job applications.
  • HR manager reviews candidate profiles, resumes, and application materials.
  • HR manager shortlists candidates based on qualifications and suitability for the position.

Interview Scheduling

  • HR manager schedules interviews with shortlisted candidates through the HRMS software.
  • Candidates receive email invitations with interview details and options to confirm or reschedule.

Interview Feedback and Selection

  • Interviewers provide feedback and evaluations for each candidate through the HRMS software.
  • HR manager reviews interview feedback and makes final hiring decisions

Training and Development

Training Needs Assessment

  • HR manager conducts a training needs assessment to identify skill gaps and training requirements.
  • HR manager gathers feedback from managers and employees through surveys or performance reviews.

Training Program Creation

  • HR manager selects training programs or courses based on identified needs.
  • HR manager creates training programs within the HRMS software, specifying details such as objectives, content, and duration.

Employee Enrollment

  • Employees receive notifications about available training programs through the HRMS software.
  • Employees browse and enroll in training programs relevant to their job roles and development goals.

Training Delivery and Tracking

  • Employees attend training sessions or complete online courses as scheduled.
  • Training attendance and completion data are recorded in the HRMS software.
  • HR manager monitors employee progress and completion rates.
